Our Company

An Industry Leading Security Company

D-SAFE FORCE SDN. BHD. is a private limited company carrying out the business of providing quality security services to commercial, industrial, residential and public sector. The company was incorporated under the Companies Act, 1957 on 04th December 2009, with its full security license conferred by virtue of Private Agency Act, 1971 Section 2 (a) and 2 (b) by Ministry of Home Affairs Malaysia.

D-SAFE FORCE SDN. BHD. strives to provide a one-stop solution to all our clients’ security needs. We believe in the Man, Method and Machine methodology in providing outstanding security services. We achieve our mission by assigning a dedicated management team for each of our client’s assignment to ensure the smooth performance of duties.

About Us

D-Safe Force is a security company based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia with the ability to provide security services to private and professional (commercial) firms nationwide.

Our security guards are highly disciplined, fit & tough, professional, legal & trained to provide the best security services for you. We offer a wide range of quality security services such as manned guarding, retailed security and close protection after properties, factories, buildings and more.
