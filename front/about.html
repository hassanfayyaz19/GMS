About Us

D-SAFE FORCE SDN. BHD. is a majority bumiputera owned company and is prospect as one of the fastest growing security company to serve a variety of clients. Our numerous prospect clients are from commercial, industrial, residential and public sector.

D-SAFE FORCE SDN. BHD. has the expertise and skills required for the ever changing security needs. Our security guards provide a visible security presence, demonstrating a broad range of skills from general guarding to highly specialised security roles. We have a team of highly experienced professionals that possess the relevant expertise to protect both you and the things you value.

We have a team of professionals with relevant experience and expertise to provide tailor-made security solutions to our clients. We are organized with standard process in place to understand and service current market demands.

Our team of security experts comprises professional with experience and trained to meet and handle challenging needs of our clients. We ensure that the security services rendered are dependable and meet high standards of professionalism.

We are fully established to offer the complete range of services backed by several aspects of security, amongst which are:-

• Unarmed Guards • Area, Site and Housing Guards

• Cash- In-Transit Services • Static & Mobile Security Patrol Services

• Commercial Investigation Services • Central Alarm Monitoring System

• Body Guards & Security Escorts • Dog Units Services

• Security Management Services • CCTV Surveillance Systems
